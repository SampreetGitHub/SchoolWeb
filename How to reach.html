St. Aloysius HSS can be accesed through all types of road transportations. The road to the school can be accesed via Vadanappilly-Thrissur road or via arunnattukara elthuruth road.
School bus services are provided for the students covering almost all major places.The routes offered are:
  • Peringottukara - vadanapilly
  • Anthikkad-Kanjani
  • Manalur-Kanjany
  • Thrissur-insight
  • Thrissur-Aranattukara
Private bus(in specific times) and auto-rickshaw services are also available.
